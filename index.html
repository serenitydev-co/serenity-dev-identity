Serenity Dev Identity

A lean foundation for a technical, precise, and distinctive brand.

This presentation defines Serenity Dev's visual core across four parts: symbol, logo, colors, and typography.

01

Symbol

The symbol is built from two complementary readings: the green mass carries the “S”, while the pink mass resolves the “D”. Together, they create a more distinctive signature than a purely geometric or purely literal mark.

02

Logo

The primary lockup pairs the symbol with the wordmark in a format suitable for LinkedIn, simple landing pages, and digital materials.
